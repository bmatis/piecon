PieCon {{ current_con.start_date.year }} Gaming Weekend

Our goal for PieCon is to have a low-stress, inexpensive gaming weekend with a community of new and old gamers that we consider friends. Because friendship is magic. And then we cast magic missile.

Who

YOU and YOUR FRIENDS are invited to PieCon! Which friends? The ones who game in any way, shape or fashion; the ones you like, and the ones who would have fun in a community of gamers. We don't need to know them; your good taste is enough for us.
